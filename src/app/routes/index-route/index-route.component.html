Welcome to Villagers!

     Villagers is a social network targeted at primary and secondary education. However, it is not a real company, and this site is not a real product. Instead, this site merely seeks to explore what such a social network would look like.
But if it's not a real site, then what does it do?
     Villagers simulates a social network by providing you, the user, access to a collection of pre-made ficitious accounts, belonging to imaginary people (let's call them villagers).
So this is just...pretend?
     Yes, essentially. Unlike a real network, a user can visit the account of any villager they come across. Once you are in their account, you will see everything as they see it, and be able to do everything they can, as if it were your own. With only one account, you may gain the perspective of teachers, parents, and their students.
