Welcome to Villagers!

     Villagers is a social network targeted at parents, teachers, and students. However, it does not represent a real company, and this site is not a real commercial product.
But if it's not a real site, then what does it do?
     Villagers simulates a social network by providing you, the user, access to a collection of pre-made ficitious accounts, belonging to imaginary people (let's call them villagers).
So this is just...pretend?
     Yes, because with imagination we can paint in broader strokes. Unlike a real network, a user of this site can visit the account of any villager they come across. Although fabricated, it allows for the experience of filling another's shoes. Are you ready to explore?
